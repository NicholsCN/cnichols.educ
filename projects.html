Projects & Initiatives

Current & Ongoing

Policy Leadership

Pennsylvania Deans' Forum

President, 2024–Present · Member since 2006

A consortium of higher education leaders from Schools and Departments of Education across Pennsylvania. As President, I facilitate the network and its ongoing engagement with key policy issues related to teacher quality and the teacher workforce, including sustained dialogue with state officials and institutional leaders across the Commonwealth.

Fellowship

Strickland Global Leadership Institute

Fellow & Designer, 2025–Present

Bill Strickland founded the Strickland Global Leadership Institute to support and expand a network of vocational training and cultural enrichment centers across the United States and around the world. As a Fellow and Designer, I am part of the inaugural cohort — contributing to the work of training social innovators and helping carry forward a vision grounded in beauty, opportunity, and the belief that education can reach those most overlooked by traditional systems.

Community & Arts

Hope Center for Arts and Technology

Executive Board Member, 2014–Present

A nonprofit in Sharon, Pennsylvania focused on free adult vocational education and youth arts programming. I have been part of this organization since its founding, contributing to strategic planning, programming, fundraising, and organizational growth — and have had the privilege of watching it become a model for community transformation through education and the arts.

Regional Institutional Partnerships

Ongoing
